### Innovation Description (for DPIIT form)

> Kosyn AI is developing an AI-powered multilingual medical scribe for Indian healthcare. The product uses real-time speech-to-text technology optimized for Indian languages (Tamil, Hindi, Telugu + English code-mixing) to transcribe doctor-patient consultations and automatically generate structured clinical documentation — SOAP notes, ICD-10 codes, prescriptions, and drug interaction alerts. Unlike global medical scribes (Abridge, Nuance) which fail on Indian accents and languages, and unlike existing Indian solutions (Eka Care) which are locked to proprietary EMR systems, Kosyn AI is a standalone, affordable medical scribe that works with any clinic workflow. The product addresses the critical documentation burden that costs Indian doctors 2+ hours daily and contributes to clinical errors from incomplete records.
