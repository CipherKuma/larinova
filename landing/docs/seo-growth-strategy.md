## Phase 4: Ongoing Growth (Month 2+)

### Doctor Testimonials as Content
- Every pilot doctor who gives feedback → turn into a case study blog post
- Format: "Dr. [Name] from [Clinic, City] saves [X] hours daily with Larinova"
- Real name, real clinic, real numbers
- These rank for local searches AND build E-E-A-T

### YouTube
- [ ] Record a 2-minute demo: "How I document 40 patients a day in Tamil"
- [ ] Upload to YouTube with keyword-optimized title and description
- [ ] Embed on homepage — increases time-on-page
- [ ] Create short-form clips for Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts

### WhatsApp & Word of Mouth
- Every doctor who searches "Larinova AI" after hearing from a colleague = branded search signal
- Branded search volume is one of the strongest ranking factors for new domains
- Create shareable content (1-page PDF, WhatsApp-optimized images) doctors forward to peers

## What NOT to Do

- Don't buy backlinks — Google penalizes this
- Don't submit to 100 random directories — focus on high-authority ones listed above
- Don't write generic "AI in healthcare" thought leadership — too competitive, won't rank
- Don't optimize for broad US-dominated keywords like "medical transcription software"
- Don't use AI-generated content without heavy editing — Google detects and deprioritizes it
